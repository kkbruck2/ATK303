Services

Upholstery

This chair was part of a set that had been in storage for years and had fallen apart. The frame was restored and broken pieces repaired or replaced. The springs suspension was retied and covered with new materials.

Refinishing

This buffet started out as a dark walnut brown color. The client had a cherry dining set that she wanted it to match. The piece was stripped, bleached, and then restained with a light cherry stain to match her dining set.

Decorating

Flea-market find that was tiled with glass mosaic tiles. Then the entire surface was encased with epoxy to create a smooth water proof surface.
